HOW TO USE

1

Wet Your Hands

Start with clean, wet hands and warm water.

2

Create Lather

Rub the soap between your palms to create a rich, creamy lather.

3

Gentle Massage

Apply the lather to your face in circular motions, avoiding the eye area.

4

Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse with lukewarm water and pat dry with a clean towel.

Use twice daily for best results
Store in a cool, dry place
